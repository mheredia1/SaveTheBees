  • One of the main solutions would be to ban the use of pesticides. Doing so would prevent the deaths of tens of millions of bees every year.
  • Another thing we can all do would be prevent the habitat of bees from being destroyed.
  • One of the best things we can do to stop the decline of the bee population would be for our leaders to take the threat of climate change seriously so the bee population is not decimated.
