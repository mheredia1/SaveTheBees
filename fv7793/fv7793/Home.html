Welcome

  • This site is all about educating the general public to stop the decline of the bee population!
  • People of all ages,gender, education and socio economic status will find this information helpful.

Problem

All around the world the population of bees are declining.

Causes

Many experts believe we are directly responsible for this decline.

Our use of pesticides have caused the bee population to decline.

We are destroying the natural habitat of the bees.

One of the biggest threats to the bee population would be climate change

Effect

The shrinking populations of bees will hurt our agricultural production.
